neither regular residual is removed before the determinant is formed.


2. EXACT CHAMBER REDUCTION
--------------------------

For m=p+r_m the chambers are

  head=[0,r_m], middle=[r_m+1,p-1], tail=[p,p+r_m].

Both base and companion coefficients obey

  a_(i,m-j)=(-1)^(i+1)a_(i,j),
  at_(i,m-j)=(-1)^(i+1)at_(i,j).

Thus tail reflection j=m-b gives, separately for R and Rt,

  R_head+R_tail
   =sum_(i,b) a_(i,b)[H_b^(i)+(-1)^(i+1)(H_(m-b)^(i)-p^(-i))],

and the identical formula with a replaced by at.  The middle chamber is kept
separate.  sol_midpoint_gate.py asserts these exact rational identities at all
assembled rows, including the central-zero branch.

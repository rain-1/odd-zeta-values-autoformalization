1. EXACT PHI/BELL SUBSTITUTION
------------------------------

For s=0,1,2 put

  r_s=(p-5)/2+s,  m_s=p+r_s.

For every 0<=b<=r_s one has 2r_s<p, so all factorials in a_(6,b) have fixed,
carry-free blocks:

  m_s!       = Phi(1,r_s),
  (m_s+b)!   = Phi(1,r_s+b),
  (2m_s-b)!  = Phi(2,2r_s-b),
  (m_s-b)!   = Phi(1,r_s-b),
  b!         = Phi(0,b),

where

  Phi(A,d)=p^A A! (p-1)!^A d!
           product_(t=0)^(A-1) E_(p-1)(t) E_d(A)=(Ap+d)!,
  E_d(x)=product_(h=1)^d (1+xp/h).

Thus, exactly,

  a_(6,b)=(-1)^m Phi(1,r_s)^4 (m/2-b)
           Phi(1,r_s+b) Phi(2,2r_s-b)
           /[Phi(0,b)^7 Phi(1,r_s-b)^7].                 (PHI-6)

The checker asserts (PHI-6) against the Bell and direct coefficients at every
b, including b=0 and b=r_s.  The remaining layers are the complete Bell layers

  a_(6-k,b)=a_(6,b) Y_k(D_1,...,D_k)/k!,  0<=k<=5,

and the companion is formed only afterward.  Every equality is exact over Q.

Verification status: PHI-6, Phi=(Ap+d)!, Bell=direct, and companion=direct all

3. (RM): CLOSED AS A VALUATION LEMMA
------------------------------------

In the middle chamber r+1<=j<=p-1, the factorial count in the closed a_6 form
has no p-factor in j! or (m-j)! and at least six p-factors in the numerator.
Each logarithmic derivative can lose at most its weight, hence

  v_p(a_(i,j))>=i,  v_p(at_(i,j))>=i.

Since H_j^(i) is p-integral in this chamber,

  R_mid,Rt_mid in p Z_p.

Using w,wt in p^(-2)Z_p gives

  p^5(wt R_mid-w Rt_mid) in p^4 Z_p,

level by level, and therefore after the three-level coefficient row.  This is
stronger than (RM).  The oracle actual row valuations at p=11,13,17 are 8,7,7.

Verification status: the factorial valuation proof is uniform; the checker
gives exact agreement and large-margin diagnostics.  (RM) is proved.

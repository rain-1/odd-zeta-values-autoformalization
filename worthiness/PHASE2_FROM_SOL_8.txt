VERDICT
-------

The uniform cubic gate is still OPEN. The obstruction is now precise.

At a root of a0, the CUB recurrence row is a common left annihilator of the
primitively scaled Q, P, and Phat solution columns modulo p. Hence the
saturated Casoratian is supposed to vanish there. (At upper roots, denominator
factors can cancel a0 in the raw closed formula.) Its quotient by a0 gives a
first-jet/rank statement, not a primitive-Q statement, and cannot by itself
select the P solution.

The exact oracle supports ordinary regularity of P, not subtraction of a Phat
singular direction. Every cubic root for 11<=p<50 passes the saturated P row;
the normalized Phat row passes too. The missing theorem is either:

  (a) LEAD at every cubic residue plus primitive-Q-at-a0-roots; or
  (b) an adjugate/connection first-jet that directly selects P.

The midpoint Phi proof cannot simply be cited for (a): its displayed fixed
block assumed 2r<p, whereas upper cubic roots have 2r>=p and introduce another
factorial chamber.

These are oracle gates only, not promoted claims.

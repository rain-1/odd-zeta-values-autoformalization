EXECUTIVE VERDICT
-----------------

The new calculation kills my July-19 description of (HW) as a three-digit
identity with two exceptional missing digits.  The cancellation is the full
weight-five jet: after p^5 normalization, the coefficients of p^0 through p^4
should vanish as one assembled identity.  The weight-five Phi/Bell expansion
therefore predicts Q_HW = O(p^5); it does not merely supply c_1=c_2=0.  Generic
order 5 is the maximal claim justified by that truncation.  The occasional
order 6 is presently surplus, not a prediction.

The data also kill the strong form of my P4.  The reflection-centre zero is
real, but neither a constant quotient by 2r+1-p nor, with the digit convention
used in dwork_hw.py, a global oddness law survives.  What remains is a fixed-
point vanishing governed by the full harmonic reflection ideal, not by one
linear factor.

Finally, order 5 for the singular head window is not equivalent by itself to
the full denominator-free descent floor.  It says that head-window extraction
preserves the complete singular Frobenius jet through weight five.  The regular
residual and the companion determinant are still separate obligations.


T1. REVISED MECHANISM AND EXPECTED MAXIMAL ORDER
------------------------------------------------

Put

  Q = p^5 Sing - rho_1 u - sigma_1 p^2 w,
  rho_1=29/28,  sigma_1=101/84.

Equations (3)-(10) do not naturally stop after the p^2 coefficient.  They give
a filtered Laurent expansion whose normalized, integral part is

  Q = C_0 + p C_1 + p^2 C_2 + p^3 C_3 + p^4 C_4 + O(p^5).

Here every C_k is the sum of both j-blocks, all Bell terms that can reach total
weight k, all pole layers L_1,...,L_6, and the u,w correction terms.  Equation
(6) through harmonic weight 5, equation (11) through total weight 5, and the
Bell polynomials Y_0,...,Y_5 are exactly the data needed to determine this
five-coefficient jet.  Equation (13) must be imposed as a relation in that
whole jet, not assigned only to C_1; likewise the shifted block-binomial
correction from (3),(6),(14) contributes across the jet, not only to C_2.

The revised identity system is therefore

  C_0=C_1=C_2=C_3=C_4=0,

after simultaneous reduction by:

  (a) the exact Phi block factorization;
  (b) collision extraction in (8)-(10);
  (c) the full-block reversal relations (13), through total weight 5;
  (d) the shifted Ljunggren/Jacobsthal relation, retained as its normalized
      defect rather than replaced prematurely by 1;
  (e) reflection and endpoint identities for the two finite b-sums; and
  (f) the six-layer Bell assembly, including the companion terms when the
      determinant rather than Q itself is being formed.

This explains Agent A's deletion result.  Each L_i may have low valuation, and
none of C_0,...,C_4 is a termwise identity.  The weight filtration belongs to
the assembled rational function.  Deleting L_2,...,L_6 destroys that rational
function and exposes a low coefficient, exactly as observed.

Why I previously saw only two missing digits: the E_M window computation was a
question about the span of characteristic-zero infinity relations.  It proved
one accessible digit and measured a two-digit deficit relative to the mod-p^3
target.  That was a limitation of the E_M certificate and of the target chosen,
not the depth of the Phi/Bell identity.  I incorrectly promoted the certificate
deficit into a mechanism with exactly two special cancellations.

Expected maximal order: generically ord_p(Q)=5.  Weight five proves the
remainder is O(p^5), provided the pipeline verifies the valuation of every
discarded collision term after normalization.  It gives no reason for C_5 to
vanish.  A proof of uniform order 6 would require the weight-six jet and new
relations at that weight.  The observed isolated order-6 cases should be
treated as extra roots of C_5 until shown otherwise.

The revised account is falsified by any one of the following:

  1. A clean p,r with exact ord_p(Q)<5.
  2. A nonzero normal form for one of C_0,...,C_4 after all exact block,
     reversal, reflection, and Bell relations are imposed.
  3. A supposedly omitted term whose normalized valuation is below 5.  That
     would show that “total weight at most 5” was not a valid precision rule in
     a collision branch.
  4. Deleting the reversal or normalized block defect leaves all C_0,...,C_4
     unchanged.  That would kill my attribution of the cancellation to those
     relations, even if Q remains divisible by p^5 for another reason.
  5. C_5 vanishes identically after a correct weight-six build.  That would not
     falsify divisibility by p^5, but it would falsify my present claim that 5
     is the generic maximal order explained by the weight-five mechanism.

Relation to full descent.  Use

  v = Sing + resid,
  rho_p = rho_1 p^(-5),  sigma_p = sigma_1 p^(-3).

Then exactly

  v-rho_p u-sigma_p w = resid + p^(-5) Q.

Thus ord_p(Q)>=5 makes the singular-window error integral, but resid is only
known at order -2.  In the determinant,

  p_n-rho_p q_n = wtilde E_v - w E_vtilde,

so the regular residual and its companion can still dominate.  Moreover

  Delta_5 = q_1 p^5 (p_n-rho_p q_n).

Consequently the mod-p^5 head-window statement is a very strong local shadow
of the weight-5 Frobenius normalization, but it is not equivalent to

  ord_p(Delta_5) >= ord_p(q_1)+2.

Head-window localization appears to lose none of the singular jet through
weight five.  It does discard the regular residual, the tilded coupling, the
q-coordinate, exceptional-prime lattice effects, and every a>1 descent.
Those must not be inferred from Q alone.


T2. CORRECTED P4
----------------

My July-19 P4 bundled three claims: a centre boost, reflection antisymmetry,
and a single linear first correction.  Only the first survives.  In particular,
the varying quotient c(r)/(2r+1-p) already kills the single-linear-mode claim.
An additional exact check with the digit convention of dwork_hw.py shows that
c(r)+c(p-1-r) is generically nonzero (for example, 40 of 41 residues at p=41),
so global antisymmetry is also false in that convention.

The corrected structural proposal is a reflection-ideal statement.  Set

  x = 2r+1-p,
  h_t = H_r^(t) mod p.

Under r -> p-1-r one has, for the relevant t<p-1,

  h_t -> (-1)^(t+1) h_t mod p.

At the fixed point x=0, the even harmonic sums h_2,h_4 vanish.  Therefore the
natural centre-vanishing ideal through weight five is

  I_ctr = (x, h_2, h_4),

with coefficients built from r and the reflection-even harmonic/Bell
generators h_1,h_3,h_5 and their allowed products.  My revised prediction is
not c=lambda*x.  It is

  c(r) belongs to I_ctr

after the Phi/Bell coefficient is reduced modulo p and the full-block
relations are imposed.  Equivalently the symbolic result should have the form

  c = x A + h_2 B + h_4 C,

where A,B,C are explicit weight-bounded expressions; they need not be constant,
even, or prime-independent before the universal rational reductions are made.
This permits varying quotients, mixed reflection parity, and sporadic extra
zeros while forcing the centre boost.

Exact falsifiable test for Agent A:

  1. Print c(r), c(p-1-r), their sum, and their difference.  This records the
     failure of the old parity claim rather than assuming a parity.
  2. Before numerical specialization, reduce the symbolic first-correction
     coefficient in the harmonic alphabet modulo the full-block and reflection
     relations.
  3. Divide/reduce it by a Groebner basis for (x,h_2,h_4), or simply collect it
     explicitly as x A+h_2 B+h_4 C.
  4. Print the normal-form remainder.  A nonzero remainder falsifies the revised
     proposal.  Zero remainder proves centre vanishing structurally.
  5. Specialize the resulting A,B,C for several p,r and compare with the exact
     determinant digit.  Agreement must hold for every residue, not only at the
     centre.

If the assembly cannot support a harmonic-polynomial Groebner reduction, use
exact finite-field interpolation as a diagnostic: interpolate C_p(x), verify
C_p(0)=0, and split it into even and odd parts.  That test is weaker and
prime-by-prime; it must not be advertised as the structural proof.


T3. ASSEMBLY ORDER AND REQUIRED INVARIANTS
------------------------------------------

The pipeline should work in a valuation-tagged truncated Laurent ring, not by
expanding giant rational numbers and asking for their valuation at the end.
Each term should carry: p-exponent, harmonic weight, j-block, pole layer,
companion flag, and collision provenance.

Recommended assembly order:

  1. Precision ledger.  For every requested output coefficient C_0,...,C_4,
     propagate backward the precision required from each factor.  Extract all
     p-collisions first.  Print the minimum valuation of every discarded
     remainder.  This is the proof that the weight-five truncation suffices.

  2. Factorial blocks.  Replace every factorial by exact Phi(A,b), retaining
     the residue block and tail block separately and splitting further at the
     digit-change thresholds in (5).  Normalize common factorial units only
     after their p-valuations are recorded.

  3. Kernel first.  Build a_6 in each chamber, then D_1,...,D_5 with collision
     terms extracted, then Y_0,...,Y_5, and finally a_6,...,a_1.  Handle the
     central zero by the direct B_j expansion; never cancel a_6 there.

  4. Summation second.  Sum in b within each chamber.  Apply b -> r-b and the
     finite harmonic endpoint identities, but keep head/tail, layer, and
     companion labels.  Boundary terms should be explicit objects, not hidden
     in a changed summation range.

  5. Form u,w,Sing and their tildes.  Only now assemble the six layers.  First
     form Q and Qtilde; then, in a separate output, form E_v,E_vtilde and the
     determinant Delta_5.  This separation will show exactly where the regular
     residual enters and prevents the false Q <=> Delta inference.

  6. Reduce last.  Reduce the assembled coefficients in the order: exact
     factorial identities; full-block reversal ideal; normalized shifted-block
     defect; finite-sum/reflection identities; E_M relations.  E_M should be
     allowed to explain C_0 but should not be used as a substitute for the
     p-adic block relations.

For each stage print these invariants:

  - exact agreement of direct a_{i,j} with Phi/Bell reconstruction before
    truncation, including the central-zero branch;
  - p-integrality assertions for every modular inverse;
  - the precision ledger and valuation of each omitted remainder;
  - coefficients C_0,...,C_5 by block and by layer, plus the fully assembled
    column; C_5 is needed as the prospective first nonzero coefficient;
  - reversal defects R_t from (13), with their normalized valuations;
  - the shifted block-binomial defect divided by p^3, never merely a Boolean
    statement that the ratio is 1 mod p^3;
  - Bell recurrence checks and the individual D_i monomial contributions;
  - boundary terms generated by every reflection or chamber split;
  - normal forms before and after each relation family;
  - Q,Qtilde, resid,residtilde, E_v,E_vtilde, and Delta_5 valuations separately;
  - the centre-ideal remainder for c, and both reflection defects
    c(r)+c(p-1-r), c(r)-c(p-1-r);
  - deletion deltas coefficientwise: C_k(full)-C_k(toggle-off), not only the
    final valuation.

Run P6 at relation level.  “Delete reversal” means replace the reversal
relations by independent full-block symbols while retaining their valuation
bounds.  “Delete block-binomial” means retain the normalized p^3 defect as an
independent symbol rather than setting it to its Phi-derived value.  Dropping
raw terms from an exact factorial is a different experiment and can produce a
meaningless failure.

The hard point will not be equation (6).  It will be symbolic summation across
the r-dependent digit chambers while preserving boundary terms, followed by
global reduction of an expression whose cancellation crosses L_2,...,L_6.
The second hard point is precision bookkeeping in collision branches: one lost
negative valuation can make an O(p^6) local remainder contribute below p^5
after normalization.  The third is the central zero, where the Bell formula
written as a_6 Y_m is singular although B_j is regular.  Expression swell is
likely, but it should be controlled by collecting in the harmonic/reversal
basis after every completed layer, never by reducing a layer to zero in
isolation.

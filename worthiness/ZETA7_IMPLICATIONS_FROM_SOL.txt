This is genuinely exciting.  The biggest implication is not merely that a new
recurrence has been found: the computation appears to reveal the internal
geometry of the M_{0,10}, zeta(7) construction.


1. THE EXPECTED RANK-FOUR STRUCTURE HAS APPEARED
------------------------------------------------

The leading coefficients q_n satisfy an apparent order-4, degree-19
recurrence, exactly the order predicted by the rank-four motive.  This extends
the striking progression

    zeta(3):  order 2,
    zeta(5):  order 3,
    zeta(7):  order 4.

That is compelling evidence for a systematic hierarchy of cellular
Apéry-type systems at higher odd weights.  The unexpectedly large degree 19
also says that rank alone does not measure the arithmetic complexity: the
zeta(7) geometry is substantially richer than a straightforward one-step
extension of the zeta(5) recurrence.


2. THE CHARACTERISTIC POLYNOMIAL HAS HIDDEN QUADRATIC STRUCTURE
--------------------------------------------------------------

The characteristic polynomial is

    chi(lambda)
      = lambda^4 - 6340 lambda^3 + 67974 lambda^2
        - 6340 lambda + 1.

It is reciprocal, so its roots occur in inverse pairs.  More strikingly, it
factors over Q(sqrt(3)):

    chi(lambda)
      = (lambda^2 - (3170 + 1824 sqrt(3)) lambda + 1)
        (lambda^2 - (3170 - 1824 sqrt(3)) lambda + 1).

This suggests a possible decomposition of the rank-four structure into two
reciprocal rank-two sectors over Q(sqrt(3)).  Possible interpretations include
real multiplication, an endoscopic splitting, or a hidden modular structure.

The factorization of the characteristic polynomial does not by itself prove
that the complete recurrence operator factors.  A high-priority next test is
therefore to factor the full Ore operator over Q(sqrt(3))(n), allowing gauge
transformations, shifts, and adjoint equivalences.


3. THE COMPUTATION EXPOSES TWO DISTINCT PERIOD LADDERS
-----------------------------------------------------

The most conceptually important finding may be that the zeta(5) phenomenon
does not generalize naively.  The evidence says that

    q_n, s_n, and Phat_n

follow the new recurrence, whereas

    P_n

does not.  Thus weight seven contains at least two genuinely different
holonomic species:

    the weight-5 descent ladder

and

    the primitive weight-7 ladder.

This makes the missing P_3 more interesting, not less.  Finding its recurrence
means isolating the primitive zeta(7) sector rather than merely extending the
descent recurrence.  The result is a concrete recurrence-level realization of
the weight filtration and residue structure predicted by the cellular motive.


4. NEW EXACT ARITHMETIC DATA EMERGES
-----------------------------------

Assuming the recurrence is correctly identified with the descent ladder, the
computation gives

    q_3    = 94357501,
    s_3    = 1396906795/3,
    Phat_3 = 232175579999/972.

Here

    972 = 2^2 * 3^5.

new arithmetic anomaly should live specifically in the primitive weight-7
coefficient P_3.

The recurrence also recovers q_3 exactly from q_0, q_1, and q_2 through its
special trailing-coefficient behavior at n=-1.  This is a particularly strong
internal consistency check on both the recurrence and the propagation
mechanism.


5. THE RECIPROCAL ROOTS REVEAL SEVERAL ASYMPTOTIC SCALES
-------------------------------------------------------

The four characteristic roots are approximately

    6329.2605,
      10.6454,
       0.0939374,
       0.000157996.

They form two reciprocal pairs:

    6329.2605  <-->  0.000157996,
      10.6454  <-->  0.0939374.

This is more informative than a single growth and decay rate.  It suggests two
asymptotic sectors.  The observed values of the descended form I''_n appear
more compatible with the 0.0939374 scale than with the smallest root.  If this
assignment is correct, the four scales may distinguish the primitive and
descended parts of the period system.

Determining the eigenspace occupied by each cellular period would clarify
which decay rate governs the primitive weight-7 form.  That, together with a
sharp denominator theorem, is what ultimately determines its Diophantine
worthiness.


6. THE LOW-COUPLING REPRESENTATION IS AN ALGORITHMIC BREAKTHROUGH
---------------------------------------------------------------

The computation demonstrates that the earlier creative-telescoping explosion
was representation-dependent.  The original full-width coupler creates an
elimination wall; the bounded-window representation makes exact dynamic
programming and holonomic elimination feasible.

This suggests a reusable strategy:

  * search for bounded-treewidth or bounded-window representations before
    creative telescoping;
  * use structurally different window systems as independent certificates;
  * predict computational complexity from coupling width rather than integral
    dimension alone;
  * apply the same search to prospective zeta(9) and higher cellular families.

This methodological implication may ultimately be as important as the
recurrence itself.  It turns a previously inaccessible high-dimensional
problem into one governed by local coupling geometry.

8. IMPORTANT CERTIFICATION CAVEAT
---------------------------------

The operator is extraordinarily well supported:

  * it was independently guessed modulo several primes;
  * it was reconstructed exactly by CRT and exact linear relations;
  * it annihilates all 74 available exact terms;
  * its order matches the motivic prediction;
  * its characteristic polynomial is reciprocal and has small exact integer

Nevertheless, annihilation of finitely many terms is not yet a proof that the

One should also distinguish palindromy of the characteristic polynomial from
a proved self-duality of the entire recurrence operator.  Establishing an
adjoint or gauge symmetry of the full operator would be a further theorem.
